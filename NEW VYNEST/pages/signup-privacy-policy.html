Understanding the roles and responsibilities of Students and Owners within the VyNest ecosystem.

# Introduction

VyNest is an online accommodation discovery and booking platform that enables students to directly book PGs, hostels, and flats listed by property owners. VyNest does not own, manage, or operate any property and functions solely as a digital facilitation platform.

01. Student Policy

1.1 Platform Role

VyNest provides students with access to property listings. We do not guarantee property condition, availability, or suitability.

1.2 Direct Booking & Fee

A booking fee is required to confirm intent. This fee does not include rent or security deposits.

1.3 Payments Outside VyNest

Rent, deposits, and utilities must be paid directly to the property owner.

Student Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate personal info
  • Review listing details before booking
  • Follow property rules and local laws
  • Maintain respectful conduct

02. Property Owner Policy

2.1 Nature of Service

VyNest enables owners to list properties but does not manage or operate the accommodations.

2.2 Listing Accuracy

Owners must ensure pricing, amenities, and availability are accurate and up to date.

Owner Responsibilities

  • Honor confirmed bookings
  • Maintain advertised standards
  • Clear communication of charges

03 Platform Rights & Limitations

VyNest may review, suspend, or terminate accounts in cases of misuse, fraud, or repeated complaints. VyNest is not liable for disputes or losses between users.

04. Acceptance of Policies

By using VyNest, students and property owners acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to these platform policies.
